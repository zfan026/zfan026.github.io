Hailing from the vibrant city of Wuhan in China, I embody the dynamic spirit and resilience characteristic of her hometown. Despite the unfortunate prejudice associated with Wuhan in the wake of the COVID-19 pandemic, my upbringing instilled in me a deep sense of passion and determination. Growing up in this bustling metropolis, I absorbed its energy and optimism, reflecting the city's youthful vigor and modernity. Just as Wuhan continues to evolve and thrive, I exude a similar vibrancy, driving my relentless pursuit of personal and professional growth. My ability to navigate challenges with grace and determination mirrors the resilience of my beloved city, embodying a spirit that refuses to be defined by adversity.

During My time as a student at UCR, I dedicated myself to gaining invaluable work experience that would complement my academic pursuits. My internship at the Hubei Province Charity Federation stands as a testament to my multifaceted skills and commitment to making a difference. Working within the Publicity and Fundraising department, I seamlessly balanced data entry tasks with my creative flair, actively contributing to poster design and event planning. My efforts played a pivotal role in amplifying the organization's message and maximizing fundraising endeavors. Furthermore, my tenure at Inner Journey, a travel company, showcased my adaptability and productivity. My role involved crafting over 20 engaging original passages and posters, demonstrating my knack for storytelling and visual communication. Through my diverse experiences, I not only honed my professional skills but also exhibited a passion for leveraging my talents to positively impact both local communities and global audiences.

My journey as a UCR student transcended beyond academic excellence, marked by my profound dedication to both my professional growth and the welfare of her fellow students. As the Student President of the Chinese Students and Scholars Association, I epitomized leadership, compassion, and cultural ambassadorship. Beyond my stellar performance in internships, I tirelessly devoted myself to enhancing the experiences of international Chinese students at UCR. My initiatives extended far beyond administrative duties, as I personally facilitated the integration of new arrivals, ensuring a smooth transition into their American journey. Through meticulously organized events like the Mid-Autumn Festival, the First UCR Chinese Basketball Match, and the Singles House Party, I fostered a sense of community and belonging among students, transcending geographical boundaries to create a home away from home. My unwavering commitment to fostering connections and celebrating cultural diversity not only enriched the university experience but also left an indelible mark on the lives of those I touched.
